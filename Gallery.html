The Humble Beginnings (1974-1979)

Louella Bakery was possibly founded as a small, family-run bakery in Bugallon, Pangasinan, following its early success in nearby towns. Focused on traditional Filipino breads like pandesal, Spanish bread, and loaf bread. Became known for its freshly baked goods and warm, home-style service.

The bakery grew in popularity within Bugallon and neighboring towns. Began offering more varieties of bread, such as ensaymada, cheese rolls, and specialty loaves. Established itself as a go-to bakery for local residents for breakfast and merienda.
