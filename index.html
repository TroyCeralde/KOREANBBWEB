3 DIFFERENT BEST IN OUR BAKERY

BEST

VANILLA CAKE

A timeless classic, our Vanilla Cake is soft, moist, and infused with the rich, aromatic essence of pure vanilla. Each bite melts in your mouth, offering a delicate sweetness balanced with a light and fluffy texture. Perfectly layered and topped with silky vanilla buttercream, this cake is a perfect choice for any celebration or a simple sweet indulgence. Whether enjoyed on its own or customized with fresh fruits and toppings, our Vanilla Cake is a treat that never goes out of style!

SPANISH BREAD

A bakery favorite, our Spanish Bread is a soft, golden-brown pastry rolled with a sweet, buttery filling. Each bite is a perfect balance of fluffy bread and a rich, slightly caramelized center, making it an irresistible treat for any time of the day. Best enjoyed warm with a cup of coffee or hot chocolate!

HOT PANDESAL

A true Filipino classic, our Pandesal is freshly baked daily to bring you the perfect combination of a soft, pillowy inside and a slightly crisp, golden crust. Lightly dusted with breadcrumbs, this warm and comforting bread is perfect on its own, with butter, or paired with your favorite spreads and fillings. A breakfast and merienda must-have!
